What's a Chiboma?

A guide to the Not-Mon, and a free colouring page!

No, really, what is it?

Chiboma is the Chimera Not-Mon, totally not a knockoff Pokémon for the fangame Pokémon Pigment (and soon-to-be Pokémon Verse)! It was once said to be a regular Not-Mon before a crystal fell from the sky and into its possession. This crystal, which soon became its token orb, granted it immense power and slowly transformed it into the legendary Not-Mon known for saving the Mythos Area-of-Residence from destruction many centuries ago… But now? Now it’s a cute little PNG for you to colour and make your own for free!

How do I use this site?

Several options for customization have been laid out below, each neatly organized into its own category. Click on one of the colour circles to apply that colour and associated marking/design to the base on the right and keep going until you’re satisfied! Each option is based off one of its official designs, from regular-ol’ Chiboma to its ethereal forms, and even its shiny! Want to get rid of an option or clear your slate without refreshing the page? Click on the red X button to clear your selection; works best with add-ons like the gem or orb! You can even name your creation using the input form below the add-ons. Nifty, eh?
